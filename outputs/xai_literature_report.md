## Practical Implication Notes
The classified literature suggests that XAI is practically relevant when AI systems influence high-impact decisions, such as clinical support, credit or risk assessment, cybersecurity monitoring, and autonomous systems. Explanations may improve communication, support accountability, and help users question model outputs. However, explanations should not be treated as proof that a model is correct, fair, or safe.
